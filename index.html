About Our Salsa

Fresh Ingredients

Made with the very best Lulu or Megamart Tomatoes, Red and Green Onions, Cilantro (or Coriander for rest of the world), Jalapenos, Garlic, Lime, Green Chiles, Cumin, Salt and a secret undisclosed ingredient.

Made to Order

Salsas are only made after recieving orders, never premade or frozen. We will make it to the spice level of your preference from mild to extra spicy.

Lasts Up to Two Weeks

Salsas should be kept refridgerated and can last up to two weeks in the fridge (but never really make it that long!). Keep it for longer at your own risk. Due to using fresh ingredients, the water content is high, so salsas need a nice stir each time they are ready to be eaten and will be restored to their normal consistency. It is still unknown if salsas can be frozen. Give it a try and let us know how it goes!
